Knowledge base and FAQs

HOME

Welcome to a knowledge base and tech guide for common requests and solutions. The purpose of this manual is for techs to have a centralized place for all troubleshooting related procedures. On the left is the site map, select any of those categories to read troubleshooting procedures. Click on the arrow to expand or collapse the navigation menu. At the bottom of each category is a Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ) section related to that category.

Due to the nature of the tech industry, best practices and standards often change, so although I will make an effort to keep all information up-to-date and relevant, it's entirely possible that parts of this manual will often be outdated. Therefore, if something seems inaccurate or obsolete, please use your best judgement and let me know so I can update it.

If you have any comments or quesitons, please feel free to contact me by email: dbraithwaite02@lbpsb.qc.ca.

FAQ

How is this different than the guide available on SSO?

Answer: The guide on SSO focuses on more than just troubleshooting procedures, it's also a guide for techs on matters unrelated to troubleshooting. For instance, there's an article on how to configure a laptop if you're a new employee, which has nothing to do with troubleshooting.
