Data Processing Technician Manual

Knowledge base and FAQs

HOME

Welcome to a knowledge base and tech guide for common requests and solutions. The purpose of this manual is for techs to have a centralized place for all troubleshooting related procedures. On the left is the site map, select any of those categories to read troubleshooting procedures and frequently asked questions(FAQs).

Due to the nature of the tech industry, best practices and standards often change, so although I will make an effort to keep all information up-to-date and relevant, it's entirely possible that parts of this manual will often be outdated. Therefore, if something seems inaccurate or obsolete, please use your best judgement and let me know so I can update it.

At the bottom of each page, you'll find the FAQs section for commonly asked questions related to that category. If you have any comments or quesitons, please feel free to contact me by email: dbraithwaite02@lbpsb.qc.ca.
